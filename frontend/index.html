    About CardioRetina-AI

    CardioRetina-AI is a research prototype that uses deep learning to analyze retinal fundus images for non-invasive cardiovascular risk assessment. The retinal vasculature shares embryological and physiological properties with the coronary vasculature, making it a potential window into cardiovascular health.

    Key Features: Hybrid CNN + Vision Transformer architecture, multimodal clinical data fusion, Grad-CAM explainability, SHAP analysis, comprehensive evaluation metrics, and ablation study support.

    Important Disclaimer: This project is a research prototype for educational and experimental use only. It is not intended for clinical diagnosis, treatment decisions, or as a substitute for professional medical advice. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional.
