# Research Prototype Disclaimer

## Important Notice

**CardioRetina-AI is a research prototype for educational and experimental use only.**

### Not for Clinical Use

This system is **NOT intended for**:
- Clinical diagnosis of cardiovascular disease
- Treatment decisions or medical interventions
- Replacing professional medical advice
- Screening patients without medical professional oversight
- Use in actual healthcare settings

### Purpose and Scope

The intended use of this project is strictly:
- **Educational**: Teaching deep learning concepts in medical contexts
- **Research**: Studying retinal-cardiovascular associations
- **Experimental**: Testing multimodal AI architectures
- **Demonstration**: Showcasing production-ready medical AI pipelines

### No Regulatory Approval

This model has **not been**:
- Evaluated by the FDA (U.S. Food and Drug Administration)
- CE marked for European use
- Approved by any medical regulatory body
- Clinically validated in hospital settings
- Tested on diverse patient populations

### Data Privacy Notice

Retinal images are considered biometric data. Users must:
- Handle all data with appropriate security measures
- Ensure compliance with local privacy regulations (GDPR, HIPAA, etc.)
- Not use real patient data without proper authorization
- Anonymize any data used for testing or demonstration

### Accuracy Limitations

The predictions from this system:
- Are for demonstration purposes only
- Should not be interpreted as medical facts
- May not generalize across different populations
- Require further validation before any real-world application

### Responsible Use

By using this software, you acknowledge that:
1. You understand this is a research prototype

4. You accept all responsibility for how you use this code
